📸 Tips for Best Results

  • ✅ Lay the sheet flat on a table
  • ✅ Good lighting — no shadows on checkboxes
  • ✅ All 4 black corner circles must be visible
  • ✅ Fill checkboxes with a dark pen (not pencil)
  • ✅ Don't fold or crumple the sheet
  • ✅ Phone camera is fine — no need for a scanner

🖨️ How It Works

  1. Go to your Task List, choose a date, and click "🖨️ Print Sheet".
  2. Print the PDF. It includes a QR code and 4 black corner circles — don't trim those!
  3. Do your tasks during the day. For each finished task, fill in the checkbox with a dark pen.
  4. Take a photo of the sheet with your phone or use a scanner. Come back here and upload it.
  5. The app reads which boxes are filled and automatically marks those tasks as done! 🎉
